Who We Are

A Home Built on
Faith & Dance

Well Dancers Orphanage is a church-based children's home in Kampala, Uganda. We care for over 25 children — orphans who have lost their parents and children living with physical disabilities — providing them with a safe home, nutritious meals, schooling, and medical care.


Our children are members of the Jump4Fun dance crew — a team that performs, competes, and brings incredible joy to their community. For these children, dance is healing, discipline, confidence, and hope all in one.


We operate under our church ministry and rely entirely on the prayers and generosity of people like you to keep our home running and our children thriving every single day.
